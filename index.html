Interactive Vernier Caliper Simulation - click to view

The project is an Interactive Virtual Vernier Caliper with an Integrated Physics Chatbot, developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to provide physics students with a practical and engaging way to learn measurement techniques. The virtual caliper allows students to simulate real-life measurements, including internal, external, and depth dimensions, with accurate readings from both the main scale and vernier scale. Its interactive interface lets users move the caliper jaws and receive real-time feedback, helping them practice and understand the concept of least count and precise measurement without the need for physical instruments. The integrated chatbot serves as a virtual tutor, answering students‚Äô queries, explaining concepts step-by-step, and providing guidance on taking accurate readings, thus enhancing conceptual clarity. By combining a realistic simulation with intelligent assistance, the project creates a self-paced, accessible, and effective learning environment that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills in physics.
